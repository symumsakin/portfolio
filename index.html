Research Experience

Diabetes Prediction using Machine Learning Algorithms

Undergraduate Thesis

This study used machine learning to predict Diabetes Mellitus from patient health records. We found that Decision Tree and Random Forest algorithms worked well across different datasets. We learned that carefully preparing the data and selecting the right features are crucial for getting accurate predictions. These findings show that machine learning could help catch diabetes early. But we still need more research to make sure our results are solid and to try out other methods that might work even better.
